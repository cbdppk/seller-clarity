Seller Clarity demo script

Problem:
Many informal sellers write sales in rough notes, WhatsApp messages, or memory. That makes it hard to quickly know what was sold, how much came in, and what needs attention.

User:
This app is for small sellers in Ghana who do not need a full accounting system. They just need quick clarity from messy daily sales notes.

Wow moment:
I paste rough notes like "Yam 10gh per each and sold 40, Rice 60 bags 1000gh". In one tap, the app turns that into clean records, totals, charts, and simple insights I can understand on my phone.

Why it matters in Ghana:
For many sellers, the phone is the main work tool. Seller Clarity helps them move from rough notes to clear decisions without spreadsheets, bookkeeping stress, or extra setup.
